Release Notes for MegaPirateNG 2.3

This version is compatible with Arduino IDE 1.0!

=== How to compile MegaPirateNG ===

1. See additional requirements for BlackVortex boards in the release_notes_BlackVortex.txt
2. Delete libraries folder in Arduino IDE folder
3. Copy libraries folder from Arduino IDE installation archive into Arduino IDE folder
4. Copy libraries folder from MegaPirateNG distributive into Arduino IDE folder
5. Select your frame, sensors type in the apm_config.h (Quad X, ALLINONE by default)
6. Select your RC Channel mapping in /libraries/APM_RC/APM_RC_PIRATES.cpp
7. For tuning the LED sequencer open the LED.pde and read the descriptions


=== Arduino board pin mapping ===

*** RC channels (can be changed in APM_RC_PIRATES.cpp) ***
* Default is TX_mwi - MultiWii set
A8	- ROLL
A9	- THROTTLE
A10	- PITCH
A11	- YAW
A12	- AUX1
A13	- AUX2
A14	- CAMPITCH
A15	- CAMROLL

*** Camera stabilization ***
d44 - Camera stabilisation Roll servo
d45 - Camera stabilisation Pitch servo

*** GPS ***
RX2 - TX on GPS
TX2 - RX on GPS

*** Telemetry (XBee, APC220, Bluetooth)
RX3 - TX on XBee
TX3 - RX on XBee

*** CLI switch ***
a5 - Enter CLI switch (tie to ground to enter CLI) 

*** Sonar ***
d9 - Sonar Tx 
d10 - Sonar Echo 

*** Status LEDs ***
d13 - RED
d30 - YELLOW
d31 - GREEN

*** LED Sequencer ***
A1,A2,A3,A4 - default LED sequencer pins (read more in led.pde),

Motors description:
B- back
R- right
L- left
F- front
U- upper
D- lower
W- clockwise rotation
C- counter clockwise rotation (normal propeller)
S- servo (for tri)

Example: FLDW - front-left lower motor with clockwise rotation (Y6 or Y4)
